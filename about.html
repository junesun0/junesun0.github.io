On Film

Film is unlike any other art form — it tells stories through the same dimensions we live in. Beneath an ocean of infinite colors and sounds lies the fourth dimension, the pulsing current of my world and yours: time. A good film transcends reality and reminds us of what it means to live.

What makes us human is the ability to share our feelings, and even more so, the willingness to feel for others. Through stories, we get to understand one another’s passion for life.

Filmmaking has grounded me in our world. It's shown me the power of people, and serves as a gentle reminder of how our impact can be multiplied exponentially with modern technology.
